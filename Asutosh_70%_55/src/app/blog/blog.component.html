From brain drain to gain: How hi-tech Hyderabad has now become talent attractor

Traditionally, Hyderabad has been recognized as a net exporter of talent, but recent trends highlight a compelling surge in talent inflow. This shift is attributed to the burgeoning opportunities arising from the establishment and expansion of Global Capability Centers (GCCs). Cities like Pune, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Delhi, and Chennai are contributing to this positive trend. Hyderabad has also developed a stronghold in certain specialized skill sets. An impressive 20 per cent of the global Pega skill sets are now centered in Hyderabad.

Peak XV leads $10 million Series A1 funding round of spacetech start-up Digantara

Bengaluru-based space situational awareness (SSA) firm Digantara Research & Technologies has raised $10 million in a Series A1 funding round to further develop its flagship Space-Mission Assurance Platform (Space-MAP), the company announced Tuesday. The funding round was led by the venture capital and growth investing firm Peak XV Partners—formerly Sequoia Capital India—with participation from Kalaari Capital, Japan-based venture capital firm Global Brain, Campus Fund and the founders of IIFL Wealth.
